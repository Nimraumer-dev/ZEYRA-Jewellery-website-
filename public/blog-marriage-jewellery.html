Matching Bride and Groom Jewellery Ideas for 2025

February 12, 2025 By Zeyra Fashion Team

Harmony, connection, and style are all about a wedding in 2025, and that is such a beautiful affair into jewellery. Bride and groom jewellery match has been one of the most significant trends that reflect unity and a shared aesthetic journey. Couples are now looking for subtle ways to complement each other without being overly identical.

For the bride, this often means intricate chokers or Rani Haars with specific stone colors—emerald greens or ruby reds. For the groom, a matching brooch, a string of pearls, or even a Kalgi that features the same stones creates a visual bridge between the two looks.

Sustainability and heritage are also key. Many couples are choosing artificial jewellery that mimics the weight and luster of heirloom pieces, allowing for elaborate designs that are both practical and breathtaking. Whether it's through color-coordinated gemstones or similar metallic finishes (like antique gold or polished silver), the goal is to create a cohesive royal appearance.

In 2025, we're seeing a rise in "minimalist coordination," where small details like cufflinks or a sherwani button set subtly echo the patterns in the bride's bangles or necklace. It's about those quiet echoes of love that make for stunning wedding photographs and cherished memories.
